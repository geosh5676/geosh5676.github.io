About

A licensed psychotherapist and developer with degrees from the University of Chicago and The European Graduate School—and additional studies at Stanford University and The Juilliard School—he brings a foundation in human relational dynamics and expressive communication to his technical work in data engineering, training curriculum architecture, and AI systems development.

His focus is on building intelligent, empathic, and capable relational guidance systems, researching socioaffective alignment, and designing training pipelines for therapeutic presence in embodied AI.

He still sees clients, couples, and runs Men's Groups—gladly.
